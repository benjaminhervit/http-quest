Game manual

This is a game where you will have to get your hands dirty with HTTP.
When you register, you party name will appear on the leaderboard and you can begin your adventure.

To complete the game you must learn the following:

  • How to make HTTP requests
  • How to request with different methods
  • How to send data through different formats
  • How to deal with bad puns, pop culture references, terrible documentation, and even worse guide instructions.

Game rules:
The most important rule is to remember to share your party_name with the Game at each step.
Beyond that there are no rules. There is no single solution to completing the game.
The Game will tell you what it accepts and what it rejects. If you beat a level it does not matter how - but we hope you can tell us why it worked.
